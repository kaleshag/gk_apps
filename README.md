## Apps

### 🍎 Health & Habits Tracker

A Progressive Web App for tracking food, calories, workouts, water intake, steps, sleep, and daily habits.

**Live App:** https://kaleshag.github.io/gk_apps/health-tracker/

**Features:**
- 📊 Calorie tracking with meal categorization
- 💪 Workout logging with calories burned
- 💧 Water intake tracking (4L daily goal)
- 🚶 Steps counter (10,000 daily goal)
- 😴 Sleep tracking (7-8h optimal)
- ✅ Daily habits checklist
- 📈 History tracking
- 📱 Works offline as PWA
- 💾 Local data storage

**Install on Mobile:**
1. Visit the live app URL
2. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
3. Use like a native app!
